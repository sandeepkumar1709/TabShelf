AI features

Toggle off to hide all AI buttons and skip every Gemini request.

What gets sent to Google
When AI is enabled, TabCache sends the following to generativelanguage.googleapis.com (Google Gemini):
  • Tab titles and full URLs for the action you triggered
  • The current scope label (e.g. currentWindow)
Nothing is sent automatically — only when you click ✨ or open the 🤖 Smart view. Requests use your API key directly from this browser; no TabCache server is involved.
